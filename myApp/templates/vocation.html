The Contemplative Life
A calling
Is God calling you?

“Listen, and incline the ear of your heart.” So begins the Rule of Saint Benedict — the same words that have opened the monastic day at Mepkin for generations.

A life of prayer and work

Our brothers rise before dawn to chant the Psalms, gather seven times a day for prayer, and give their hands to honest labor — tending gardens, keeping bees, and welcoming guests. It is a hidden life, ordinary and luminous.

Could this be your path?

A vocation rarely arrives as certainty. It begins as a quiet question that will not leave you. If these words stir something within you, we would be honored to listen with you.
